About the Author

Adam Gifford is a Fellow from The Data Incubator's Winter 2021 cohort and IC-U is his culminating capstone project from the fellowship. Prior to fellowship, Adam received his PhD in neuroscience from the University of Pennsylvania in 2016 and was a postdoctoral researcher for the past 3 years at the University of Louisville. Adam currently lives in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area with his wife, Jasmine, and their 4-year-old Bernese-lab mix, Bo.
