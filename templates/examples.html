Stable patient

A stable patient is one that IC-U predicts will not need intensive care within 5 days of hospital admission.

Questionable patient

A questionable patient is one that IC-U predicts will need intensive care between 1 and 5 days from hospital admission.

Urgent patient

An urgent patient is one that IC-U predicts will need intensive care between 1 and 24 hours from hospital admission.

Immediate patient

An urgent patient is one that IC-U predicts will need intensive care less than 1 hour from hospital admission.
